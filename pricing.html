FAQ

Can I change my plan later?

Yes — modules can be added or removed at any time; the price is recalculated from the next billing period.

Is there a free trial?

Yes, 7 days of full access to every service. No credit card required.

Where is my data stored?

Each workspace is a separate, isolated deployment with its own database and mail in the Reneos cloud; your data is never mixed with other customers’ data. Encryption in transit and at rest, with regular backups. For enterprise, a dedicated on-premise installation in your own environment is available on request.

How does migration work?

Every service ships with import tools: mail and calendars from Google Workspace and Microsoft 365, files from cloud storage, projects and issues from your current tracker.
