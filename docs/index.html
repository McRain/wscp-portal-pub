Why Reneos instead of Google Workspace or Microsoft 365

The same toolkit for your team — but your data, your domain and control stay with you.

An isolated environment per customer

No shared multi-tenant database: every customer gets its own database, its own mail and its own sign-on. Different companies’ data never sits side by side.

Your domain, your data

Mail and services run under your company domain. Your data stays isolated in your own instance and never trains anyone else’s models.

No vendor lock-in

A lightweight open stack and standard protocols (IMAP/SMTP, CalDAV, OIDC). Migrate and take your data with you whenever you want — no lock.

We deploy and run it

Managed end to end: domain setup, updates and backups are on us. You get a ready workspace in minutes instead of administering servers.
